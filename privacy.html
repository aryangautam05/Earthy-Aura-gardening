Privacy Policy

Our commitment to protecting your personal information and respecting your privacy in all your interactions with Earthy Aura Gardening.

Introduction

At Earthy Aura Gardening, your privacy is of paramount importance to us. This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of personal information received and collected by our website, how we use it, protect it, and your rights related to your data.

Information We Collect

We may collect various types of information including but not limited to:

  • Personal identification information (Name, email address, phone number, etc.)
  • Usage data, such as pages visited, time spent on pages, and clicks
  • Cookies and tracking technologies to improve user experience

All information is collected with your consent, or as necessary to provide our gardening services effectively.

Use of Information

The information collected is used exclusively for the purposes including but not limited to:

  • Providing and maintaining our gardening services
  • Communicating with you regarding your inquiries and orders
  • Improving user experience and website functionality
  • Sending you updates, newsletters, or marketing materials if you opted in
  • Compliance with legal obligations

Data Protection

We adopt appropriate data collection, storage, and processing practices and security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information, username, password, transaction information, and data stored on our site.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information
  • Withdraw consent to processing where applicable
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Request data portability
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us via the contact details at the end of this document.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your browsing experience and analyze website traffic. Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help remember your preferences and usage.

You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect website functionality.

Detailed Information About Our Data Policies

Our Privacy Policy is detailed and comprehensive to ensure transparency and to comply with applicable laws such as the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for EU residents, California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and other relevant privacy regulations depending on your location.

Types of Personal Data Collected

We collect personal data that you voluntarily provide to us when registering on the website, placing an order, filling forms, subscribing to newsletters, or contacting customer service. This may include:

  • Full name, mailing address, email address, phone number
  • Payment information necessary to process orders, which is handled securely via our payment processors
  • Service preferences and any special requests or instructions
  • Feedback and reviews you submit about services or website experience

Non-personal data that may be collected automatically includes:

  • IP address and browser type
  • Operating system and device type
  • Browsing activity and interaction with the site

How We Use Collected Information

The collected information is utilized to:

  • Process transactions and deliver services requested by you
  • Improve customer service and respond efficiently to inquiries
  • Personalize user experience and enhance usability
  • Manage marketing campaigns including personalized offers and promotions
  • Analyze website traffic and usage to improve our platform
  • Prevent fraudulent activity and ensure website security

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist in operating our website and conducting business, provided they agree to keep your information confidential.

We may also disclose your information when legally required to comply with law enforcement or governmental requests, or to protect our rights, property, or safety.

Data Retention

Your personal data is retained for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

Security Measures

We use a combination of technical, administrative, and physical safeguards including SSL encryption, firewalls, secure servers, access controls, and regular audits to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Third-Party Links and Services

Our website may contain links to external sites or services. We do not control and are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

International Users

Your information may be processed or stored in countries other than your own. By using our services, you consent to the transfer and processing of your information in countries which may have different data protection laws.

Cookies and Tracking Continued

We use cookies for purposes such as remembering your preferences, improving navigation, and enabling certain features. Types of cookies we use include:

  • Strictly necessary cookies
  • Performance and analytics cookies
  • Functionality cookies
  • Targeting and advertising cookies

Our cookie policy is designed to comply with relevant regulations, and you may control cookie usage via browser or device preferences.

Your Privacy Rights

Under various laws, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right of Access: You may request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Right of Rectification: Request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data.
  • Right to Object: Object to data processing based on legitimate interests or direct marketing.
  • Right to Data Portability: Obtain and reuse your data across different services.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: If processing is based on consent, you can withdraw it anytime.

To exercise these rights or make any inquiry, please contact us at the contact information below.
